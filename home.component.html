About

Khan Puri graduated from AUT with a Bachelor of Health Science in Podiatry. Prior to undertaking his career in Podiatry, he completed his Certificate in Fitness Instruction from AUT.

He enjoys all aspects of podiatry work but has a special interest in treating and managing diabetic foot disease. He was awarded the AUT Joe Spring award in 2016 for orthotic therapy. Khan also has a keen interest in musculoskeletal disorders and sports injuries augmented by his background as a fitness instructor and has a certificate in Sports Massage.

He is also committed to further education and an evidence-based patient care approach for optimal outcomes. Outside work, Khan loves to go to the gym and keeps updated with the latest research trends in foot & ankle disorders and takes every opportunity to attend seminars and conferences to expand his knowledge base.

Languages spoken: English, Hindi and Punjabi

What is Podiatry?

Podiatry is the field of medicine which deals with the diagnosis & treatment of feet, ankle or lower leg. The Podiatrist's scope of practice includes areas such as paediatrics, diabetes, sports injuries, structural problems, treatment of the elderly as well as general foot care.

Common conditions a podiatrist would treat include bone joint disorders, muscular, calluses, corns,skin & nail disorders and sporting injuries.
